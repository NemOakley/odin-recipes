Cheeseburger Recipes

Description

Here we have an image of a tasty looking cheeseburger .

Ingredients

  • american cheese
  • hamburger
  • pre-cooked bacon
  • burger buns
  • lettuce
  • condiments

    • Steps

      What you'll need to make this pizza

  • First, you will need to shape your hamburger into proportional patties
  • Next, you will need to put them on a pan or grill and cook each side for about 2-3 minutes depending on the size of the patty
  • While cooking make sure to add some spices that you like to give a little extra flavor to the meat
  • After cooking the meat, toast the buns by adding a little butter and apply heat
  • After toasting the buns, put your condiments of choice on the buns
  • Then, put your cheese on top of the burger while it is still warm, this way the cheese will melt on
  • Next, put your lettuce on and apply the top bun to the burger
  • Eat and enjoy
