Pizza Recipes

Description

Here we have an image of a NY style bacon pizza. The characteristics of the pizza that make it NY style are the thin crust and the crunch that can be hear when taking a bite. This pizza will be a nice and cheesey joy to have, why sit here and read this recipe webpage when you can get started cooking.

Ingredients

  • pizza dough
  • pizza sauce
  • pre-cooked bacon
  • pizza cheese

    • Steps

      What you'll need to make this pizza
    1. Firstly, you will need some pizza dough. This can be store bought or made from scratch, for beginners we recommend purchasing some from a local store.
    2. Next, you should spread out the dough into a circle; making sure not to spread too thin or leave the dough too thick
    3. Next, you will need some pizza sauce, which you should spread out around the dough using a spoon. Make sure to leave about an inch of dough uncovered around the edges, that will be your crust.
    4. After spreading the sauce, you should sprinkle some cheese onto the pizza, mozzarella should work just fine. Go heavy or light, it is all personal preference.
    5. Next, add the bacon. Personally one can never add too much, but adjust to your preferences
    6. After the pizza has been assembled, make sure the oven has been heated to an appropriate temperature, such as 350 degree fahrenheit.
    7. After 15 minutes have passed or the crust appears golden brown and cruncy, your pizza is ready to be pulled out of the over and consumed after a 3 minute cooling process.
    8. Enjoy!!!
