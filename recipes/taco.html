Taco Recipes

Description

Here we have an image of a standard taco, known for its quick craftability.

Ingredients

  • taco shells
  • mexican shredded cheese
  • shredded lettuce
  • cooked hamburg
  • taco seasoning

    • Steps

      What you'll need to create this taco

  • Firstly, you will need to cook your hamburger if you have not already .
  • After cooking the meat, make sure to season the taco with your choice of taco seasoning
  • Next, you will need to put the shells into the over and cook at 375 for 3 minutes.
  • After the shells are done, it is time to assemble the Taco
  • Add the meat into the shell
  • Followed by the cheese.
  • Lastly, add the shredded lettuce with a light dusting of cheese on top
  • Enjoy!!!
